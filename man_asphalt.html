Asphalt Plant

Asphalt plant is located in Baalbeck - Bekaa used for manufacture of asphalt mixture s, that have been used in pavement construction . Sometimes collectively known as black top or Asphalt Concrete . Its asphalt mix can be widely applied for the pavement of high ways and municipal roads. It is composed of cold aggregate ,supply system ,drum dryer ,coal burner , coal feeder , dust collector ,hot aggregate elevator , vibrating screen , filler supply system , weighing and mixing system ,asphalt storage ,bitumen supply system. Due to its accurate weighing system for dosing the aggregate bitumen , and other mixtures .Although, it is characterized by energy saving ,environmental protection ,safe and ease to operate . Our products are of high quality and according to the standards of The Ministry Of Public Works. Capacity range 100-120 TPH.
