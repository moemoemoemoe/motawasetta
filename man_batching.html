Batching Plant

Batching Plant

Our batching Plant is specialized in manufacturing high-quality concrete used in building ,roads ,concrete pipes and manholes …etc.

It is consists of several parts and accessories that combine and work together to produce high quality concrete they are :

Aggregate feeders:

It is one of the most important component .Several bins are located side by side to facilitate feeding of coarse aggregate and sand .

Aggregate weigh conveyor :

It is used to weigh aggregates one by one and then transfer the same to charging conveyor skip . Aggregates are discharge to the weigh conveyor by opening and closing of pneumatic cylinders.

Charging Conveyor :

It is used to carry weighed aggregates to mixing unit

Cement Weigh Scale :

It will hold the cement transferred from the silo/ hopper via screw conveyor . Its task is to weigh the aggregate prior to dispatch into the mixing unit.

Water Weigh Scale :

It receives water from the water pump,hold it and weigh the water as per recipe set in the Control Panel then add to the mixing unit.

Additive Weigh Scales:

Are used o weigh additive material prior to dispatch into the mixer .

Cement Silo/Hopper With Screw Conveyor :

it holds large volumes of cement provided with safety accessories And environmental filter . A screw conveyor is at the bottom enabling transfer of cement in cement weigh Hopper.

Mixing Unit :

It is the heart of the concrete batch mixing plant . It receives all ingredients and mix them thoroughly for fix time .Then it will discharge the contents into a transit mixer or a concrete pump.

Air Compressor :

It is used to control all pneumatic operations of the concrete batch mixer like opening and closing of cylinders that control the feeder gates ,opening and closing of valves of the mixing unit gate.

Control Panel :

It will control the working o f the machine . Control Panel assist in fast and accurate measurements of the ingredients. With growing demand for high quality and accurate concrete to produce quality material without any wastage. OPERATION –WORKING OF BATCHING PLANT

ALMOTAWASETTA BATCHING PLANT depends on five primary functions as mentioned below :

• Aggregate feeding =====> Aggregates and sand have to be fed into bins.

• Powder feeding =====> Cement ,fly ash and additives-components of binding material.

• Water =====> Water will help cement bind with aggregate . Specific quantity of water is must for any ready mix.

• Storage and conveyance =====> referring to the storage of cement ,fly ash ,etc. they have to hold up quantity in bulk and transfer small quantity by screw conveyor to the weighing hoppers.

• Control System =====> It is very critical for any advanced machine .It makes operations fast and accurate.

Concrete Batching plant process will start from the feeding of aggregates into the bins.These aggregates will be weighed separately as per the design set in the control panel .After weighing , they will be transferred to the mixing unit there are weigh hoppers for cement , additive and water .Then , the conveyor transfer the contents into the mixing unit. Cement is transferred to the weigh hopper .Water is pumped up into the weigh hopper .After mixing for a specific time the mixer will discharge the contents into transit mixer.

All the processes are carried out carefully, professionally and systematically within specific time frame needed for each process. Laboratory and field testing meet the international requirements for BS,ASTM And DIN concerning strength ,leakage and absorption.
