Who We Are

Al Motawasetta S.A.R.L for Trading and Contracting Co

is a contracting company based in Lebanon with a branch in Qatar (Medyar). The company is registered in Lebanon as grade 1 contracting company specialized in infrastructure works with a successful story from 2007 up to date. In that period Al Motawasetta succeeded in leaving an impressive footprint in the Lebanese contracting market as well in the region.

Al Motawasetta

has worked with numerous governmental agencies and multinational organizations on turnkey projects including but not limited to Sewer networks, Potable water networks and Tele communication Construction ,Electricity and line transmission ,Waste sorting Plants Dams and Lakes. We firmly believe it is the combination of our people, machines and passion that makes it possible for us to be the best at what we do . With the right combination of experience professionalism and dedication .AlMotawasetta strengthen from project to project and it is poised to face new challenges for the rapid changing industry ‘People, power and passion to build on’, that’s our statement and our strength.

Brief About Al Motwasetta By CEO

From a modest beginning in 2007, to becoming a leading contractor in Lebanon, the emergence of Al Motawasetta evolved through a journey that has taken just over a decade of planning, hard work and dedication. This commitment has made Al Motawasetta a recognized brand for the successful delivery of numerous prestigious projects in the country . As we go above and beyond on every project and deliver on our promises with integrity . Al Motawasetta is firmly committed to Quality, Health, Safety and the Environment. We continue to invest in qualified talent and modern equipment to undertake large and complex projects , which require a combination of our activities and services to deliver the optimum result for our client depending on professionalism-passion ,team work ,experience and responsibility to deliver quality on time and safety protecting the environment. This enables us to achieve our goal of successfully delivering large-scale projects and expanding our operations into the region, where we hope to replicate our local success. We are proud of our achievements and are committed to further enhancing our capabilities to ensure that we remain at the forefront of the industry, responding to the needs of Lebanon and the region.
