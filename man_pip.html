Why ?

Why you should use concrete drainage ?

Sustainability

Concrete pipes outperform other types of pipeline solutions in a number of the environmental impact categories such as human toxicity levels and chemical hazardous waste generated. The CO2 emission from concrete and cement production are relatively small compared to other building materials.

Strength

Concrete is much more durable than any kind of pipe.Hence, it can carry more load at any given time and gain strength over time.It can’t be weakened by heat,moisture, mould or pests,nor it will rust.Under ground Concrete Pipes have the ability to resist chemical attacks and massive impacts.

Quality

High quality reinforced and non reinforced pipes and manholes according to international standards ASTM.

Whole life value

Due to it’s amazing structural properties and functional benefits precast concrete pipes tend to attract lower insurance premiums than those built from other construction materials. The natural strength of precast Concrete Pipes enables recycled aggregate to be used in bedding materials,significantly reducing installation costs and the elimination of waste disposal costs.

Fire Resistance

Concrete forms a highly effective barrier to fire spread and it doesn’t emit any toxic fumes when effected by fires. It will not produce smoke or drip molten particles .Therefore concrete can be described as virtually”fireproof”.Due to its in built fire resistance properties. Concrete not only maintains an air right construction that stops smoke spreading,but also has the ability to keep its strength during fire.

Bedding performance

The superior strengthof the concrete pipes enablesrecycled aggregate to be used as a bedding material, thus reducing costs and environmental empact during installation .

Chemical Resistance

Concrete Pipes and manholes are resistant to sulphate and chemical attack.

Thermal mass and Operational Energy Efficiency

Heavy weight materials such as concrete provide a high level of thermal mass , a useful property which helps to regulate the temperature within structures. It also has agood acoustic properties, which avoids the need for acoustic insulation of pipes..
