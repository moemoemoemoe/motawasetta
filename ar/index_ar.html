من نحن

Al Motawasetta L.L.C for Trading and Contracting Co. (Formally Al Houssam Trading and Contracting)

is a contracting company based in Lebanon with a branch in Qatar (Medyar). The company is registered in Lebanon as grade 1 contracting company specialized in infrastructure works with a successful story from 2007 up to date. In that period Al Motawasetta succeeded in leaving an impressive footprint in the Lebanese contracting market as well in the region.

Al Motawasetta

has worked with numerous governmental agencies and multinational organizations on turnkey projects including but not limited to Sewer networks, Potable water networks and Tele communication Construction ,Electricity and line transmission ,Waste sorting Plants Dams and Lakes. We firmly believe it is the combination of our people, machines and passion that makes it possible for us to be the best at what we do . With the right combination of experience professionalism and dedication .AlMotawasetta strengthen from project to project and it is poised to face new challenges for the rapid changing industry ‘People, power and passion to build on’, that’s our statement and our strength.
